My Story

Hey, my name is Alberto, but my friends call be Berto. I'm from Fresno; born and raised, and wouldn't want to be anywhere else. That's why I went to Fresno State to get my Multimedia Degree. It's also why I started to learn things like HTML5, CSS, Bootstrap and Flexbox at Geekwise Academy.

I want to use my skills to help make Fresno great. I want to take pictures of downtown and make videos about its people. I want to write about the town's struggles and document its triumphs. I just want to be here and be a part of the city i love.
